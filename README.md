Hotel Management and Hospitality Information System

Overview

The Hotel Management and Hospitality  is a database-driven solution developed using Microsoft SQL Server to automate
and improve hotel operations. The system provides an integrated platform for managing guests, rooms, reservations,
billing, payments, staff assignments, and service requests while improving efficiency and reducing operational errors.

---

Problem Statement

Many hotels and hospitality businesses continue to rely on manual records, paper files, and spreadsheets to manage their daily operations. This often results in several challenges, including:

Double booking of rooms.
Inaccurate billing and payment calculations.
Delays during check-in and check-out processes.
Poor tracking of room availability.
Difficulty generating management reports.
Lack of automated business rule enforcement.

These challenges reduce operational efficiency and negatively affect customer satisfaction.



Proposed Solution

This project provides a centralized database solution that automates hotel operations and eliminates manual processes by:

Managing guest information electronically.
Automatically tracking room availability.
Preventing double reservations.
Generating bills and payment records automatically.
Producing operational and financial reports.
Supporting management decisions through dashboard reporting.

Innovation

Data Reporting Dashboard

The major innovation of this project is the implementation of a **Data Reporting Dashboard** that converts hotel operational data into meaningful business insights.

The dashboard provides:

Room occupancy analysis.
Monthly revenue reports.
Reservation trends.
Payment status reports.
Guest activity reports.
Room utilization statistics.

Benefits of the Dashboard

Supports management decision-making.
Improves operational monitoring.
Reduces manual reporting activities.
Provides real-time business insights.
